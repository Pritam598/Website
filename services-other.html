Influencer connect

A curated connect and marketing segment that helps people and businesses reach influencers on social media platforms for their campaigns and announcements.

The team helps you clarify the kind of reach you want, prepare the creative, and match you with suitable influencers instead of sending generic messages.

  • Aligning timelines so your message goes out at the right moment.

Printables

Printable designs

Asta Epsilon Infotronics designs posters, logos, flyers, brochures, and other printable assets that carry the same clean, industrial feel as the rest of your brand.

Designs are prepared for real-world printing: correct sizes, bleed where needed, and exports in formats that work with common printers and online print services.

Because the team also builds websites and software, the visual language can stay consistent across your online and offline presence.

  • Event and festival posters for campuses or venues.
  • Logos for clubs, startups, or personal brands.
  • Flyers and handouts for campaigns or launches.
  • Brochures and one-pagers for products or services.
Pricing
Printable design services

Entry pricing for small, simple assets. Larger campaigns, sets, or complex illustrations will be scoped and priced with you.

Deliverables typically include print-ready files (PDF/PNG) and, where possible, editable source files.
